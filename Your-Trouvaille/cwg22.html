CWG 2022 / Birmingham 2022

Detailed Itinerary (Departing ASR)

Day 01 Amritsar to London

06:40 Students must arrive at the Amritsar airport, meet tour manager at the airport & then catch flight to London. Meals included in flight.
09:55 Flight departs from Amritsar airport.
11:10 Arrive at Delhi Airport. Proceed for de-boarding & tour-manager will guide students to connecting flight.
14:00 Flight departs from Delhi Airport.
16:55 Flight arrives at London Heathrow Airport, follow tour-manager for group immigration.
17:45 Post immigration & collection of bags meet our local representative at meet & greet point. He will be standing with School placard. Meal: Packed Snacks
18:30 Depart from airport, upon arriving at the hotel proceed for group check-in. Free time to relax.
20:30 Pickup and proceed for dinner at multi-cuisine restaurant. Post dinner transfer back to hotel. End of services for the day

Day 02 London to Birmingham

07:00 Make your way to hotel restaurant for breakfast
09:00 Check-out from hotel
09:30 Depart from London hotel & proceed for transfer to Birmingham en-route 2 hour Guided tour of Oxford (walking)
11:30 Arrive at Oxford meet your guide and proceed for the tour with visit to Chirst Church Dining Hall
13:30 End of guided tour. Proceed for local lunch
14:30 Pickup and proceed for Stratford-upon-Avon
15:30 You are now arrived at the birth place of Shakespear & proceed with entry to the museum.
16:30 Continue journey to Birmingham. Meal: Packed Snack
18:00 Arrive at Birmingham and proceed for check-in to hotel. Free time to relax.
20:00 Proceed for Indian dinner
21:00 Transfer back to hotel. End of services

Day 03 Birmingham

08:00 Breakfast at hotel restaurant
09:30 Pickup from hotel and proceed to visit University / School in Birmingham with lecture on stream Science.
11:30 Free time to interact with students & teachers at the Uni / School.
13:00 Lunch at the Uni / School
14:00 Post lunch visit to Cadbury Chocolate Museum
16:00 Proceed to visit Think Tank (Science Museum)
17:30 Meet your guide and proceed for 02 hour guided tour of Birmingham city.
19:30 End of tour at Indian restaurant for dinner.
21:00 Pickup and transfer to hotel. End of services for the day.

Day 04 Birmingham & T20 Women

08:00 Breakfast at hotel restaurant
09:30 Pickup from hotel and proceed to visit University / School in Birmingham with lecture on stream Maths.
11:30 Free time to interact with students & teachers at the Uni / School.
13:00 Lunch at the Uni / School
14:00 Pickup and proceed to visit Jaguar Manufacturing Unit
15:30 Pickup and proceed for visit to Edgbaston Stadium for Women T20 Match. Meal: Packed Snack
20:30 Pickup and proceed for dinner
21:30 Pickup and transfer to hotel. End of services
